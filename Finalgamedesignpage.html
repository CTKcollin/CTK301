Game Desin 2 Final Project

During my semester enrolled in CTK352 - Game Design 2, I Learned some of the basics of the Unity game design engine. utilizing terrain tools and donwloaded assets I created a small section for a map to be used in a game. Where the player walks through some brief forested locations to get to a cabin on a small island in the map center. every last tree, rock, and pebble was meticulously placed with player experience in mind (including the giant spoon, don't judge me you're lucky there's only one spoon). I used this assignment to go all out in my first digital model. one that I plan to expand on in the future to hopefully add to a complete functional game.

Crab Bucket! the hit, classic board game that you know and love!

Made in my Game Desin 1 class at ISU as my first project. Myself and a wonderful team of four worked very hard to expand on this ambitious project at the end of the semester in designing the final piece which is Crab Bucket. Crab Bucket is a fully playable board game with complete Art assets created by me, you can see some of the cards I designed on the right. Art assets weren't all that were needed to make the game. Through weeks of design changes and multiple playtests, what started as a small concept quickly became increasingly a more balanced and fun player experience. Our group realized The potential this game has and we gave it our all to make it as complete as possible before semesters end. Thats not all though, once the first print of Crab Bucket comes in this fall I plan to get the team back together to further polish and eventually publish Crab Bucket for sale! If you'd like to play the game this fall send me a message through one of my contacts. If you like it hopefully some day you can pick up a copy for yourself!
